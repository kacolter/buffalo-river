Wild Buffalo River Company

Heritage, Nature, & Community

Mission Statement

We are dedicated to providing outdoor enthusiasts with an environmentally responsible gateway to the wilderness of the Buffalo National River.

Our Story

Located in the heart of Ponca, Arkansas, Wild Buffalo River Company was born from passion for the buffalo national river- America’s first national river. We aren’t just outfitters; we are stewards of the Ozarks.

Our Commitment

Inclusivity & Sustainability

We believe the outdoors belong to everyone. Our services are designed to be accessible to seasoned paddlers and first time visitors alike. The sustainability rule we practice is a “Leave no trace” principle to protect the river’s ecosystem

Local Quality

We exclusively use Buffalo canoes, locally manufactured in Newton County, ensuring you have the most reliable equipment on the water.

Why Wild Buffalo River Company ?

We offer a seamless digital booking experience paired with deep local expertise. Whether you are here for the world-class elk watching in Boxley Valley or a peaceful float through the Ozarks, We provide the tools and the gear to make your trip unforgettable.
