Bear Ridge Cabin

Experience the ultimate retreat in our premier cabin, designed for those who want to immerse themselves in nature without sacrificing comfort.

Key Features

  • Main floor and sleeping loft with queen-size bed
  • Complimentary WIFI
  • Fully furnished kitchen with dining bar
  • Stone walk-in shower in bathroom
  • Hot tub and stone firepit on patio

Booking your stay

Our mobile-friendly platform allows you to view availability and book your stay at Bear Ridge with confidence. Bear Ridge offers two guests a cozy, fully furnished cabin for a romantic getaway in upper Buffalo River country.

The cabin has a main floor and sleeping loft with queen-size bed. We are pleased to offer complimentary WIFI with this cabin rental. The cabin's kitchen features a dining bar and is fully furnished with appliances, basic meal prep utensils and cookware, a coffeemaker and even a dishwasher. In the cabin's bathroom, you'll find a lovely stone walk-in shower, along with towels, soap, and shampoo.

Amenities

Every stay at Bear Ridge includes the following as standard:

  • Hot tub and stone firepit on patio
  • Private creekside sitting area (Coming soon!)
  • Wifi (no cell service in the area)
  • 42” TV with satellite/local channels, BluRay player, movies
  • Fully-equipped kitchen – microwave, dishwasher, coffeemaker, toaster
  • Dishes, pots and pans, cleaning supplies
  • All bed and bath linens
  • Front and back porches with seating
  • Outdoor gas grill
  • Mini-split HVAC plus electric “fireplace” heater
  • Parking in front of cabin
  • Firewood for outdoor fire pit
  • Convenient to the Buffalo Outdoor Center store and the Ponca Elk Education Center
  • Just a short drive to the Ponca Access and the Lost Valley Trailhead
